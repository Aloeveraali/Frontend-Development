Coraline

Coraline (2009) is a stop-motion animated dark fantasy film directed by Henry Selick, based on Neil Gaiman's novella. It follows Coraline Jones, a young girl who discovers a secret door in her new home, leading to an alternate, seemingly perfect world. However, this world is controlled by her sinister "Other Mother," who wants to keep Coraline there forever by sewing buttons into her eyes. The film's eerie visuals and dark themes explore bravery, loneliness, and the dangers of seeking perfection, as Coraline learns to appreciate her real life and family.

The characters in the movie Coraline are

  • Coraline Jones
  • The Other Mother
  • Charlie Jones (Coraline's Father)
  • Mel Jones (Coraline's Mother)
  • Other Father
  • Wybie Lovat
  • Mr. Bobinsky
  • Miss Spink and Miss Forcible
