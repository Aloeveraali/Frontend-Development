My favorite Recipe

One of my favourite food to make is Quesdilla. They are delcious and very easy to make

The ingredients that are needed are:

  • 1 pound (350g) cheddar cheese
  • 1 pound (350g) shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup (120ml) sour cream
  • 1/2 cup (120ml) shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 cup (120ml) shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup (120ml) grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 cup (120ml) sliced fresh mushrooms
  • 1/2 cup (120ml) sliced fresh green onions
  • 1/2 cup (120ml)
    Here are simple steps to make a quesadilla:
  1. Heat a pan: Place a non-stick pan over medium heat.
  2. Add tortilla: Place a flour tortilla in the pan.
  3. Add fillings: Sprinkle cheese and any other desired fillings (like cooked chicken, veggies, or beans) on half of the tortilla.
  4. Fold: Fold the tortilla in half over the fillings.
  5. Cook: Cook for 2-3 minutes until the bottom is golden and the cheese is melted, then flip and cook the other side for another 2-3 minutes
  6. Slice and serve: Serve the quesadilla on a warm plate, and enjoy!
